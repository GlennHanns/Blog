Background

The long version

Glenn Hanns ACS is a Sydney-based Director of Photography known for intimate, visually rich storytelling across feature drama, television series, documentary, short film, music video and commercial work — with well over 150 projects to his name.

His AFTRS short Bleeders was nominated for Best Cinematography at Camerimage, Poland (Student Etudes category) and screened at Festival de Cannes (des Antipodes). An accredited ACS cinematographer and AACTA member, his films have screened internationally — from Camerimage, Cannes and Zurich to Beijing, Schlingel, Cinemagic, San Sebastian and the Adelaide Film Festival.

Recent credits include the psychological creature feature The Room Below (dir. Kurt Martin, starring Alyssa Sutherland and Jonathan Rhys Meyers). Works have been distributed worldwide on:

His approach is rooted in visual authorship — not just camera operation, but the full creative and technical leadership of the image from prep through to the grade.
